VIRAT KOHLI

INDIA

King Kohli

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is one of the most celebrated cricketers of his generation. Renowned for his aggressive batting style and exceptional consistency, Kohli has set numerous records across all formats of the game. He made his international debut in ODIs in 2008 and quickly rose through the ranks, making his Test debut in 2011 and his T20I debut in 2010. Kohli became the captain of the Indian Test team in 2014 and took over the ODI and T20I captaincy in 2017. His leadership and batting skills have earned him several accolades, including being the fastest player to reach 8,000 to 12,000 runs in ODIs and numerous ICC awards. In the Indian Premier League (IPL), Kohli has been a key player for Royal Challengers Bangalore since 2008, holding records for the most runs in a single season. Known affectionately as "King Kohli" and "Chase Master," he is admired for his ability to perform under pressure and his commanding presence on the field. Beyond his cricketing prowess, Kohli is also recognized for his commitment to fitness, philanthropy, and his influence in inspiring the younger generation.
